Aatto is a shepherd of a specific type of Spriteling, the Lunaling. Tought by a mysterious ancestor, Aatto once played songs on a magically crafted flute to call to the lunar beings. Unfortunately, his instrument was broken and the Lunalings will remain asleep until he can awaken them with his song.
