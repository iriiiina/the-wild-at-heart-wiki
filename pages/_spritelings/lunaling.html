--- layout: post title: "Lunaling" description:

The Lunaling is the rarest and most mysterious form of Spriteling currently known. Originally thought to be Will-o'-the-wisp or Hinkypunk, its luminescent composition gives it increased strength at night, allowing Lunalings to lift objects of great weight. Immune to various forms of corruption, their affinity for lunar magic also allows them to interact with ancient Lunastones, illuminating otherwise dark areas. It has even been rumored that Lunalings can fend of Nevergazers themselves, when threatened...

Lunalings can only be summoned by a shepherd trained in these Spritelings' ancient musical rites.
