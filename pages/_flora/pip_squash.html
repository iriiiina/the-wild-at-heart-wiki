--- layout: post title: "Pip Squash" description:

These unique plants are more than just your average flora - they are the vessels from which the seeds of young Spritelings grow. Using the pips that drop from these gourds, Spritelings can be hatched from a Spirit Well.

It seems the type of pips dropped by a squash will be largely influenced by the type of Spriteling hitting it.
