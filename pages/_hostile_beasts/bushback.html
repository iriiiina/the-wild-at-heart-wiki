--- layout: post title: "Bushback (or Shelterhorn)" description:

The Shelterhorn is a beetle-like creature that spends most of its days partially submerged underground, consuming any insects that may dig near it. To remain undisturbed, the Shelterhorn has evolved a patch of flora on its back to camouglage it with the appearance of a bush. Though it may seem placid, when sensing a nearby threat the Shelterhorn is a very fast and aggressive foe, attacking with the large horn on ots nose.

However, like similar creatures with bulbous protuberances on their backs, if tipped over the Shelterhorn will struggle to right itself. A local Greenshield rumor even says a Shelterhorn was once flipped by a strong gust of wind.
