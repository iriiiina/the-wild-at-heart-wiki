The Gruggthud is a mysterious, ancient creature made of pure, sentient stone. Not accustomed to company, however, the Gruggthud is very territorial and perhaps one of the most formidable residents of the Deep Woods. Often containig treasures and rare materials in the branches on its back, great rewards are possible for the few that are able to lay a Gruggthud low.
