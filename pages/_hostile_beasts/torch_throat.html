--- layout: post title: "Torch Throat (Toad)" description:

A variant of the Bloat Toad with flame properties. Instead of excreting "Crud" the Torch Throat produces flames, and will leave a patch of burnt soil when it leaps and lands. Only Spritelings with a strong resistance to heat should attempt to engage the Torch Throat.
