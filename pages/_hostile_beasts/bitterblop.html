--- layout: post title: "Bitterblop" description:

The Bitterblop can be found in the frigid, high altitude areas of the Deep Woods. When leaping and landing, the Bitterblop produces disruptive (but breakable) pillars od ice. Its damaging frost aura is easily ignored by Spritelings with an innate resistance to cold.
