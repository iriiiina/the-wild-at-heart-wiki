--- layout: post title: "Piggy Bank" description:

This gadget will keep loose scrap fastened down tight! Simply keep one on hand and you won't lose scrap from hits or from gainting. It is fragile though, and will break after enough damage.

Made by combining a bunch of parts with something sturdy.
