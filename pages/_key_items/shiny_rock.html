How To Get The {{ page.title }}?

Grey Coat gives a Shiny Rock to Wake when they first met in the Deep Woods. So you don't have to search for it.

How To Use Shiny Rock?

Grey Coat said about using Shiny Rock: "I usually just picture the person in my head and then laudly SCREAM their name into the crystal. That seems to work some of the time!"

When Wake used the Shiny Rock for the first time, he asked "Does it always feel this weird?". Grey Coat answered to this: "You will eventually become used to the side effects of the mind link, probably!"
